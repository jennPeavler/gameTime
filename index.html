Welcome to Breakout

How to Play

You have 3 lives to complete your mission, BREAK DEM BRICKS!

  1. Press Spacebar to launch the ball off the paddle.
  2. Break bricks to score points.
  3. Don't let ball get passed the paddle, it'll cost you 1 life.
  4. Break all the bricks to pass the level
  5. Each level, the speed of the ball increases and the length of the paddle decreases
  6. See how far you can go!!!
